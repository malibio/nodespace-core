Color Scheme Analysis

Most Professional

Warm Gray: Clean, corporate feel. Great for business environments and long reading sessions.

Easiest on Eyes

Soft Green: Natural, calming. Reduces eye strain during extended use.

Most Modern

Cool Slate: Contemporary tech aesthetic. Popular in developer tools and design apps.

Ultra Minimal

Monochrome: Pure focus on content. Maximum clarity and contrast.

All schemes are designed to work seamlessly with light/dark mode switching and maintain excellent accessibility standards. Try the dark mode toggle above!
